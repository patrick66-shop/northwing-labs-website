## 13. Messaging Rules

1. Lead with business problems and outcomes before technology details.
2. Use plain business language.
3. Avoid unnecessary technical jargon.
4. Do not fabricate customer results, testimonials, logos, or metrics.
5. Clearly label internal products, demonstrations, concepts, and client work.
6. Keep the tone practical, confident, clear, and direct.
7. Do not imply NorthWing Labs is a large agency.
8. Emphasize direct partnership and practical problem solving.
9. Use live HTML text for all production copy.
10. Do not place critical copy inside images.

---

## 14. Homepage Message Summary

The homepage should leave the visitor with these conclusions:

1. NorthWing Labs understands small-business operational problems.
2. NorthWing Labs can build custom software, AI tools, automations, mobile apps, and SaaS products.
3. The focus is practical business improvement, not technology for its own sake.
4. The process is clear and manageable.
5. The company can demonstrate real product and system-building capability.
6. The next step is a conversation about the business problem.
