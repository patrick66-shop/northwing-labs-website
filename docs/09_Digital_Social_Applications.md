# 09 — Digital and Social Applications

## Website

Maintain consistency across:

- Header and navigation
- Hero section
- Service cards
- Process sections
- Case studies
- CTA sections
- Forms
- Footer

Use the logo, color, typography, icon, and UI systems exactly as defined in this guide.

## Social Media Templates

Create reusable templates for:

- Company announcement
- Service spotlight
- Educational tip
- Workflow before-and-after
- Product or project showcase
- Case study
- Testimonial
- Call to action

## Social Profile Image

Use the approved square NorthWing Labs profile logo.

Do not place the full horizontal logo inside a small social avatar.

## Social Sharing Card

Recommended default copy:

**NORTHWING LABS**  
Helping Your Business Take Flight.

Custom Software • AI Tools • Business Automation

Recommended visual treatment:

- NorthWing Midnight background
- Reversed logo
- Subtle technology or workflow pattern
- Limited Flight Blue accents
- Small Flight Gold directional element

## Consistency Principle

The website, social media, proposals, and future software interfaces should look like parts of the same brand system rather than separate design projects.
