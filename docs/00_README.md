## Brand Summary

**Brand:** NorthWing Labs  
**Tagline:** Helping Your Business Take Flight.

NorthWing Labs helps small businesses use custom software, AI tools, business automation, mobile apps, and SaaS products to save time, reduce manual work, improve efficiency, strengthen customer experiences, and support growth.

The brand should feel:

- Practical
- Modern
- Capable
- Clear
- Approachable
- Results-driven
- Technically credible

The visual identity should avoid generic futuristic AI aesthetics, excessive complexity, and decorative technology imagery that does not support a clear business message.
